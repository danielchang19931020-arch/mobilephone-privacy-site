Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application ("PulseGrid") handles user information.

Information Collection

The App does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

However, third-party services used in the App may automatically collect certain technical information.

Advertising Services

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks.

These services may collect limited information including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Country or region
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA if permitted)

This information is used only for delivering advertisements and improving ad performance.

App Tracking Transparency

In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, the App will request permission before accessing the device's advertising identifier (IDFA).

Users can choose whether to allow tracking. If tracking is not allowed, the App will continue functioning normally and may display non-personalized advertisements.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 16 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information used by the App.
